My name is Wilhelmina Onyothi Nekoto, an African woman and former professional basketball and rugby player.
I traded the rugby boots and pitch for an academic background in Software Engineering, training in Radio Astronomy and strong interests in AI for Good.
I maintain a permanent residency atMasakhane, a grassroots initiative to put African languages on the global NLP map.
Here I explore cool ways to create speech and text datasets fo the critically endangerd Khoekhoegowab and widely spoken Oshiwambo.

As a friend of the wildlife, My interests are rooted in culture and narrowed to Deep Learning for Computer Vision, and NLP especially for biodiversity, language and heritage conservation.

I am currently leading a personal project on text and speech dataset creation for the widely spoken Oshiwambo, with veterans and Job Seekers,In Namibia.
