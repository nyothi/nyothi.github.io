My name is Wilhelmina Onyothi Nekoto,
I have a Bachelor of Information Technology in Software Engineering, from NUST, Namibia.
NLP Researcher at Masakhane A grassroots initiative to put African languages on the global NLP map.

My interests are Deep Learning for Computer Vision, and NLP especially for biodiversity conservation.

I am currently leading a personal project on text and speech dataset creation for the widely spoken Oshiwambo with veterans and Job Seekers,In Namibia.
