My name is Wilhelmina Onyothi Nekoto,
I've a Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering, from NUST, Namibia.
I am a final-year Master Student in Data Engineering at Jacobs University Bremen, Germany.

My interests are Deep Learning for Computer Vision, and NLP with a special crush on Healthcare.

I am currently a Drug Discovery Research Intern at Mila Quebec AI Institute under the supervisions of Yoshua Bengio and Dianbo Lui. Previously, I was also a NLP Data Scientist Intern at Roche Canada, working on Health/Pharma-related challenges.

Alternatively, I am working on NLP language technologies, with a focus on low-resourced Sub-Saharan languages at Masakhane, and Google AI (as a Graduate Student Researcher).

I am the co-creator of the FFRTranslate, and Okwugbe ASR (Automatic Speech Recognition for low-resourced languages) Python library
.
